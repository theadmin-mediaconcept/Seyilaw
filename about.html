The Founder

Meet Owoeye
Oluwaseyi Samuel

Founder & CEO
Owoeye Oluwaseyi Samuel
Founder, CEO & Lead Instructor
Owoeye Oluwaseyi Samuel is the visionary founder and CEO of Sheyilaw Computer Institute. Driven by a deep passion for technology, education and community empowerment, he established the institute in 2020 with a clear mission: to make professional digital services and computer education accessible to every Nigerian, regardless of background or experience level.

Under his leadership, Sheyilaw has grown from a single-room operation into a full-service digital and educational hub, serving hundreds of students, families and businesses across the community. He personally oversees every service and training session, ensuring each client receives dedicated, high-quality attention.

Oluwaseyi believes that when people have access to the right tools, knowledge and support, they can achieve anything. His hands-on approach and genuine care for clients is what sets Sheyilaw apart.
Access to digital knowledge is not a luxury — it is a right. My mission is to ensure no one in this community is left behind in the digital age. — Owoeye Oluwaseyi Samuel, Founder & CEO
